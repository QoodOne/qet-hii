Let's Qet Hi!

You have to Download MetaMask

What is this?

Qet Hi! is a Web3 project that let you customize/randomize simple "Hi!" images and mint them as ERC721 tokens. All the artworks are SVG images stored in metadatas as plain text on Polygon Blockchain. So no external storage required for artworks.

In short they are all on-chain NFTs which will live on as long as Polygon Blockchain exists.

How can I Qet Hi!

You have to ...

  1. Download MetaMask.
  2. Set up your MetaMask wallet.
  3. Add and switch network to Polygon Mainnet.
  4. Buy or transfer some MATIC (Polygon native token) to your wallet.
  5. Click "Login With MetaMask" button and sign message (no gas fee required).
  6. Customize or randomize and preview your Hi! image (Chrome or Brave are better for color picking).
  7. When finish customizing, click "Mint Your NFT" button, the MetaMask popup window will show you minting price + gas fee.
  8. After MetaMask confirms the transaction, wait a couple minutes. Then login to OpenSea or Rarible with MetaMask and you could see your newly mint NFT there.
  9. Then you could showoff, transfer or trade your NFT freely.
  10. Have a good one!

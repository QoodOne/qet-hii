Hint

In order to mint your NFT, you have to

  1. Download MetaMask.
  2. Set up MetaMask wallet. It's so IMPORTANT to write down your Password and Secret Backup Phrase mindfully and keep them somewhere safe, because you could not recover or import your wallet to new devices without them.
  3. Add and switch network to Polygon Mainnet.
  4. Buy or transfer some Polygon native tokens (MATIC) to your wallet.
  5. Click "Login With MetaMask" button and sign message, no gas fee required.
  6. Randomize or customize and preview your Hi! image above. (Use Chrome or Brave for better color picking.)
  7. When you like what you see, click "Mint Your NFT" button, you'll see minting price plus gas fee.
  8. After the transaction is confirmed, wait a couple minutes and login to OpenSea or Rarible with MetaMask then you could see your newly mint NFT there.
  9. You could then transfer or trade your NFT freely.
